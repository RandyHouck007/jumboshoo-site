How Jumbo Shoo Works

From a single proof-of-concept box to a modular, networked virtual fence, Jumbo Shoo has evolved through field trials into a scalable architecture for coexistence.

From JS1 to JS2 and beyond

  • JS1: Proved the core idea — geophone, camera, thumper, and solar in one unit — and exposed power and maintenance limits.
  • JS2: Split the system into swappable sensing, deterrent, and compute modules linked by a shared power and data bus.
  • JS3 (vision): A true virtual fence: many low-power sensor nodes stitched into an intelligent network that protects entire corridors.

Three-layer architecture

  • Perimeter: Off-grid sensor nodes listen and watch for elephants, 24/7.
  • Verification: Cameras, thermal imaging, drones, and collars confirm species and behavior.
  • Response: Lights, infrasound "thumpers," alerts to rangers and communities, tuned to minimize stress and risk.
