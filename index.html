Engineering a Virtual Fence for Elephants & People

Jumbo Shoo is a field-tested, modular system that detects elephants early and gently turns them away from farms and villages — before conflict begins.

Developed with Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University and ERP (Elephants, Rhinos & People), it brings full-stack engineering to human-elephant coexistence.

Support ERAU Students Support ERP.NGO

At a glance

Goal: Detect elephant herds in time to protect crops and lives using non-lethal, autonomous technology.

Where: Rural landscapes in Southern Africa with no grid power or cellular coverage.

How: Seismic and acoustic sensing, edge compute, LoRa telemetry, and a ladder of gentle deterrents.
